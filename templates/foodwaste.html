1. Tweet us your surplus

You're busy and don't want to spend ages online updating websites or making calls. So just tweet @foodtradeHQ including '#surplus' and we'll spread the word.

2. We'll match you to local projects & businesses

We're building a database of who can use what, where. And it's not just community projects who can use surplus. Startup businesses can turn surplus into livelihoods and healthy food, too.

You can: - say who you're working with - tweet in requests, Or responded to local opportunities - engage new members and businesses - subscribe for updates (actually that's coming soon) - put yourself on the map with a profile

3. In real time, brilliantly

And because it's connected to the rest of the FoodTrade database for real-time food trading, we can help streamline logistics, distribute large volumes between many people, and unlock more potential.

Numerous industries have been radically transformed by ‘disruptive technology’, but to date there has been little technology innovation in the grocery industry which can be considered truly disruptive. That may all be about to change…
