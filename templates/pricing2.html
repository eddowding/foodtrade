  Will FoodTrade include more features in the future?
Yes. We will continuously develop features, for both Pro and standard customers. Pro users actually help us shape the roadmap for future developments by suggesting and voting on feature requests.

Will you continue to invest in the free version of FoodTrade?
But of course. We will continue to add new features for both paid and standard accounts. Having the paid version is a way to give early access to specific features for and give our community makes us more sustainable and enables us to innovate faster.
