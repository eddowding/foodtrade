Common thoughts right about now

Can I ask for a refund?

Yes. If you try FoodTrade and are not satisfied, you have 30 days to ask for a refund. Refunds are not available on the 'Snack' plan.

Will FoodTrade include more features in the future?

Yes. We will continuously develop features, for both Pro and standard customers. Pro users actually help us shape the roadmap for future developments by suggesting and voting on feature requests.

Will you continue to invest in the free version of FoodTrade?

Of course. We will continue to add new features for both paid and standard accounts. Having the paid version is a way to give early access to specific features for and give our community makes us more sustainable and enables us to innovate faster.

How can I help you to make this better?

We created this trade network because we want to help make sure responsible businesses earn a fair return. If you have ideas about how we can do this better, please get in touch or use the feedback tab on the right.
