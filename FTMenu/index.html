Hassle-free compliance

A simple menu offers 10,000s of potential allergen entries. With the automatic allergen labeller, you can have a printed compliant menu in minutes, leaving you to concentrate on what you do best. And if your supplier changes something? We'll notify you immediately.

Better food, more customers

We’ll connect you with your suppliers, and you can find the best ingredients from what’s around you. You can even showcase your sustainability with local supplier lists and provenance maps. This means more customers walking through your doors.
